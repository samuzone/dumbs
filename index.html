HOW TO BUY :

CREATE A WALLET

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play for free. Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to Phantom.



GET SOME SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $DUMBSOR. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.



GO TO RAYDIUM

Connect to Raydium. Go to Raydium in google chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $DUMBSOR token address into Raydium, select $DUMBSOR, and confirm. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
